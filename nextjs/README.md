# UpHealth - AI-Powered Health Platform

UpHealth is a comprehensive AI-powered health platform designed to revolutionize personal healthcare through advanced technology and predictive analytics.

## 🏥 Core Features

### 🧠 Migraine Risk Prediction
- **95% Accuracy**: Advanced AI algorithms analyze symptoms, triggers, and patterns
- **Early Warning System**: Predict migraine attacks before they occur
- **Personalized Triggers**: Identify and track individual migraine triggers
- **Preventive Strategies**: AI-generated recommendations to prevent attacks

### ❤️ Heart Risk Assessment
- **Comprehensive Analysis**: Evaluates cardiovascular health using multiple factors
- **Risk Scoring**: Advanced algorithms assess heart disease probability
- **Lifestyle Integration**: Considers diet, exercise, stress, and medical history
- **Preventive Recommendations**: Personalized strategies for heart health

### 🔬 Disease Detection & Diagnosis
- **Symptom Analysis**: AI-powered symptom evaluation and pattern recognition
- **Preliminary Diagnosis**: Advanced algorithms provide initial assessments
- **Medical Consultation Booking**: Seamless integration with healthcare providers
- **Treatment Recommendations**: Evidence-based treatment suggestions

### 🥗 Personalized Diet Plans
- **AI Nutritionists**: Custom meal plans based on health conditions
- **Dietary Restrictions**: Accommodates allergies, preferences, and restrictions
- **Health Goal Integration**: Tailored plans for weight management, heart health, etc.
- **Nutritional Guidance**: Comprehensive nutritional education and support

### 📊 Health Monitoring
- **Real-time Tracking**: Continuous health data monitoring and analysis
- **Progress Insights**: Detailed reports on health improvements and trends
- **Personalized Recommendations**: AI-generated health optimization suggestions
- **Family Health Management**: Multi-user support for family health tracking
